  • Visited the British School

    In this IPC unit, students learned about the history of toys, materials needed to make toys, how to create toys, and how to design rules of play for the toys. They interviewed the other class over Zoom to ask about what kind of toys they would like and at the end of the unit, the two classes came together to play!

    A 4th grade girl is standing in front of camera to video conference
    Find out MORE

  • Beautiful Collaboration

    Using skills learned in Design class, some Year 7 Global Service Leaders built a loft for the British School Foundation classroom. Their goal was to build a sturdy, safe, and fun space for younger students to be able to read and to roleplay. Collaboration was key both for project management and the construction of the loft. The Foundation students love using their creativity and imagination to decorate this new space.

    A senior girl is carpenting
    Find out MORE
